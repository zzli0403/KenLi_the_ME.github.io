Yanjan USA LLC Engineer Internship

June – September 2025 | New Hampshire, USA

Overview

At Yanjan USA, I worked as a maintenance and process engineering intern, contributing to the reliability and optimization of high-speed manufacturing lines producing nonwoven materials.

Key Responsibilities

  • Performed diagnostics and preventive maintenance on automated production stations.
  • Repaired air lines, pumps, and motors to minimize downtime and enhance efficiency.
  • Developed an Excel troubleshooting guidebook for issue tracking across production zones.
  • Audited spare-part inventory and assisted in standardizing documentation practices.

Skills Gained

  • Hands-on familiarity with pneumatic systems, maintenance planning, and quality control.
  • Improved ability to document, communicate, and standardize industrial maintenance processes.
  • Enhanced understanding of manufacturing reliability and teamwork under operational pressure.
