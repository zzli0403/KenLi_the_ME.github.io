Bosch XC-CT EHW Technician Internship

July – August 2023 · Shanghai, China

Overview

During my internship at Bosch, I supported the XC-CT EHW team by performing diagnostic and technical tasks that emphasized precision, documentation, and collaboration between engineering departments. This position exposed me to the process of verifying automotive electronic modules and working within Bosch’s rigorous quality system.

Key Responsibilities

  • Reworked and debugged printed circuit boards (PCBs) through soldering, wiring, and functional testing.
  • Used an oscilloscope to analyze voltage signals, confirm component behavior, and assist engineers in fault tracing.
  • Translated and standardized technical documentation for bilingual teams across departments.
  • Supported diagnostics and final verification of electrical subassemblies for automotive control units.

Example of a PCB board and soldering work setup used during diagnostic procedures.

Skills Gained

  • Hands-on experience using oscilloscopes, multimeters, and soldering tools for precision diagnostics.
  • Enhanced cross-disciplinary communication between mechanical and electrical engineers.
  • Learned structured problem-solving, documentation discipline, and attention to quality assurance in production environments.
