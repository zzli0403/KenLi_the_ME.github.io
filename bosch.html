Bosch XC-CT EHW Technician Internship

July – August 2023 | Suzhou, China

Overview

During my internship at Bosch, I supported the XC-CT EHW team by performing diagnostic and technical tasks that emphasized precision, documentation, and communication between engineering departments.

Key Responsibilities

  • Reworked and debugged printed circuit boards (PCBs) through hands-on testing, soldering, and wiring.
  • Translated and standardized technical documentation for cross-functional use.
  • Assisted engineers in fault detection and verification of electrical subassemblies.

Skills Gained

  • Improved cross-disciplinary communication between mechanical and electrical teams.
  • Developed strong documentation and testing discipline for quality assurance.
  • Enhanced understanding of how precision manufacturing integrates with embedded systems.
